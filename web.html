About Turney Supermarket

Welcome to Turney Supermarket, Dominica's revolutionary new way to shop for groceries. We combine the ease of online ordering with the speed of a drive-through, allowing you to pick up your weekly shop without ever leaving your car.

Our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality products and a seamless shopping experience for the modern, busy individual. We are committed to serving our community with speed, efficiency, and a friendly smile.
